Hertz Chair in the Transdisciplinary Research Area
on Mathematics, Modelling, and Simulation of Complex Systems
University of Bonn

Our group is working on mathematical optimization and algorithm design to tackle computational questions arising in a wide range of areas, with a particular focus on problems at the interface of economics and computation. We combine methods from discrete and continuous optimization, computer science, game theory, and economics to develop efficient exact and approximation algorithms, and understand the limits of computability.
